# Music Session Brief

Collect these only when they materially affect the output.

## Core Questions

- What is the deliverable: song draft, topline, arrangement plan, lyrics, stems, mix notes, or rehearsal notes?
- What is the target feel in plain language?
- Which reference tracks or artists define the direction?
- What are the fixed constraints: key, tempo, vocalist range, instrument list, deadline?

## Musical Anchors

- Hook location and strength.
- Groove and subdivision.
- Harmonic density.
- Dynamic arc across sections.
- Production aesthetic: dry, intimate, wide, aggressive, polished, live, electronic.

## Useful Output Shapes

- Section-by-section arrangement notes.
- Chord and melody sketch.
- Lyric concept with motif options.
- Revision notes mapped to timestamps or song sections.
